# Agri Retail Sales & Inventory Analytics

## Project Overview
This data analytics project analyzes agri-retail sales, inventory movement, product demand, and regional performance to help a business improve stock planning, reduce mismatch losses, and identify high-growth product categories.

This project is designed for **Data Analyst / Business Analyst / Operations Analyst** roles and demonstrates skills in SQL, Python, Excel-style analysis, dashboarding, KPI design, and business storytelling.

## Business Problem
Agri-retail companies often face:
- Stockouts in high-demand regions
- Overstocking of slow-moving products
- Revenue leakage due to order mismatch
- Poor visibility into category-wise and region-wise performance

## Objectives
- Track monthly revenue, orders, and profit margin
- Identify best-performing regions and crop-input categories
- Detect inventory mismatch and stockout risk
- Recommend business actions to improve planning accuracy

## Tools Used
- Python: pandas, matplotlib
- SQL: joins, aggregations, window functions
- Power BI / Excel dashboard design
- KPI and business insight generation

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Gross Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Average Order Value
- Stockout Risk %
- Inventory Mismatch %
- Region-wise Sales Contribution
- Category Growth Rate

## Business Insights
1. Fertilizer and seed categories contribute the highest revenue share.
2. Some districts show strong demand but low inventory coverage, indicating stockout risk.
3. Overstocking is visible in slow-moving pesticide SKUs.
4. Regional demand planning can reduce mismatch and improve working capital efficiency.

## Recommended Actions
- Create district-level reorder alerts.
- Prioritize fast-moving SKUs before peak crop season.
- Use monthly sales trends to improve procurement planning.
- Track mismatch rate as an operations KPI.
