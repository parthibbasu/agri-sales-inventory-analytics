# Project Summary for LinkedIn / Resume

## Project Title
Agri Retail Sales & Inventory Analytics

## One-Line Summary
Analyzed agri-retail sales and inventory data to identify revenue trends, stockout risk, order mismatch, and district-level performance opportunities.

## Skills Demonstrated
SQL, Python, pandas, matplotlib, KPI analysis, business insights, inventory analytics, dashboard planning.

## Business Impact Framing
The project shows how analytics can support better stock planning, reduce mismatch losses, and improve regional sales visibility for agriculture-focused businesses.
