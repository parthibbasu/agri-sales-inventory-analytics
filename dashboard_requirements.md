# Dashboard Requirements

## Dashboard Title
Agri Retail Sales & Inventory Performance Dashboard

## Pages
### 1. Executive Overview
- Total Revenue
- Gross Profit
- Profit Margin %
- Total Orders
- Mismatch Rate %
- Stockout Risk %

### 2. Regional Performance
- Revenue by State
- Revenue by District
- District-wise mismatch rate
- District-wise stockout risk

### 3. Product & Category Analysis
- Category revenue contribution
- Top products by revenue
- Units sold by category
- Slow-moving inventory products

### 4. Operations Risk View
- High stockout-risk products
- Overstocked SKUs
- Mismatch trend by state
- Suggested reorder priority

## Suggested Visuals
- KPI cards
- Bar chart: Revenue by category
- Line chart: Monthly revenue trend
- Map: State/district sales
- Matrix: Product-level inventory health
- Conditional formatting for stockout risk

## Recruiter Talking Point
This dashboard converts raw sales and inventory data into clear operational insights for procurement, regional sales planning, and SKU-level inventory control.
